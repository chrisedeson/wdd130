CHRISFLEX WHITE WATER RAFTING

At Christopher's Rafting, we're on a mission to deliver exhilarating and safe rafting experiences led by seasoned professionals.

HISTORY

Founded in 2010 by river guide Christopher Edeson, Christopher's Rafting started with one raft and a vision to share the thrill of whitewater adventures. Over the years, it grew into a premier rafting company, known for its expert guides, commitment to safety, and unforgettable experiences. Today, Christopher's Rafting continues to lead the way in outdoor adventure, providing exhilarating trips while preserving the natural beauty of the rivers they love.
